PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 2026-03-02

1. Information We Collect

NIKKI may process and store technical identifiers including: Server IDs, User IDs, Channel IDs, Role IDs, configuration settings, and command usage logs required for functionality.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect passwords, direct message content, payment details, or any sensitive personal information.

3. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed solely to provide bot functionality, moderation systems, logging features, and service improvements.

4. Data Storage

Data may be stored on secure hosting infrastructure. Reasonable technical safeguards are implemented.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained while the Bot remains in a server or as necessary for operational integrity.

6. Data Deletion Requests

Server owners may request deletion of stored data by contacting us.

7. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

EU users may request access, correction, or deletion of stored data in accordance with applicable regulations.
